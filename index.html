5 Amazing Facts About Lions

  1. Male lions can weigh up to 420 pounds!
  2. Adult lions can eat up to 69kg of meat a day.
  3. Lions can run at speeds of up to 50 miles per hour in short bursts
  4. Back in ancient times, lions roamed every continent.
  5. Female lions do the majority of the hunting for the pride.
